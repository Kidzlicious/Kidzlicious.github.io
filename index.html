Hungry about Us!

Empowerment

Kidzlicious Kitchen (KLK) empowers children living in impoverished neighborhoods by building and implementing functional kitchens throughout the community at large. These functional kitchens will be used to teach children about the different aspects of nutrition. They will learn to grow food with urban-farming and how to prepare their own nutritious meals. 

Commitment

Appreciating our kids and honoring their well-beings triumphs when unhealthy eating is eradicated, “commitment” reinvented, and KLK is created to restore the mind, body and spirit of children living in high poverty neighborhoods. 

Partners

Harlem Grown (HG) is an independent, non-profit organization whose mission is to inspire youth to live healthy and ambitious lives through mentorship and hands-on education in urban farming, sustainability, and nutrition. We operate local urban farms; increase access and knowledge of healthy food for Harlem residents; and provide garden-based youth development programs to Harlem youth.

KLK stumbled upon HG in the Spring of 2015. KLK proposed an opportunity to collaborate with HG’s summer camp programming. The Visionary Founder/Chief Executive, Octavia A. Ruballo, met Mr. Tony Hillery, the Founder/Director in his charming garden located in Harlem, NY at the Pre-Launch of the HG's Garden and Hydroponic Greenhouse. For the first time, KLK discovered a treasure trove of eager and beautiful children whom genuinely enjoyed each and every single bite of magical, healthy goodness.
